## ğŸ¯ **Design Principles**

### **Visual Hierarchy**
1. **Hero title** - Largest, most prominent
2. **Search bar** - High contrast, easy to find
3. **CTA buttons** - Bright colors, clear actions
4. **Features** - Balanced, scannable
5. **Stats** - Eye-catching numbers

### **User Experience**
- **Mobile-first** design approach
- **Clear visual feedback** on interactions
- **Consistent spacing** throughout
- **Accessible color contrast** (4.5:1 minimum)

### **Modern Trends**
- **Gradient backgrounds** and buttons
- **Glassmorphism** effects (backdrop blur)
- **Micro-animations** (hover, focus states)
- **Rounded corners** (12px-20px radius)
- **Subtle shadows** for depth
